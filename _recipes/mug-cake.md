## Mug Cake ##

### Ingredients: ###
- 1/4 cup + 1 1/2 tsp flour
- 2 Tbs sugar
- 1/4 tsp baking powder
- 2 Tbs melted butter
- 3 Tbs milk
- 1/2 tsp vanilla

### Method: ###
1. Mix dry ingredients
2. Mix in wet ingredients
3. Microwave 70-90 seconds
4. Let rest 1 min

### Notes: ###
Add chocolate chips for fun
